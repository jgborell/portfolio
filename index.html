About Me

Thank you for visiting and welcome to my website.

My name is John Borell and I am a Python Software Developer. For the past 10 years I have been troubleshooting and repairing communication equipment for the Department of Defence. This career has been spent both in and out of the military. While troubleshooting hardware and repairing electronics is fun, software is THE PLACE TO BE!

I am currently attending codingcamp.us to sharpen my full stack web development skills. Through software development and continued studies, I plan to enhance my abilities to design new possibilities within our world of technology.

The life and culture within the developer community is what I have been longing for my entire professional career. I am looking forward to the challenges ahead, and I am grateful for the opportunities available.
